100 Years of Excellence

Celebrating a century of academic excellence, leadership, and unforgettable memories at Gamini Central College, Nuwara Eliya.

1926

The founding of Gamini Central College marked the beginning of a journey dedicated to education and excellence.

1950s

The school expanded with new buildings and programs, nurturing future leaders.

1970s

Sports and cultural activities flourished, bringing national recognition.

1990s

Technology and modern education systems were introduced.

2020s

The school celebrates 100 years of excellence and continues shaping future generations.
