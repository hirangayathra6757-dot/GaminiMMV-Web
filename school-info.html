Message from the Principal

Welcome to Gamini Central College, Nuwara Eliya. Our college stands as a symbol of discipline, excellence, and service. For generations, this institution has shaped young minds with knowledge, character, and values that prepare them for the future.

As we celebrate our journey, we remain committed to empowering our students in academics, leadership, sports, and cultural excellence. We believe education is not only about achievement, but also about building responsible citizens and compassionate human beings.

I invite all students, parents, teachers, past pupils, and well-wishers to continue supporting the growth of this great institution as we move forward into a stronger and brighter future.
